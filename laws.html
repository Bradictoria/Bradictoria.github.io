Foundational Laws

  • Law I: Loyalty to the State — All citizens shall owe allegiance to the Sovereign State of Bradictoria and its Emperor.
  • Law II: Respect for the Constitution — The Constitution is the highest legal document and shall be observed by all.
  • Law III: Protection of Civic Harmony — Acts of violence, discrimination, or unrest are prohibited and punishable by law.

Civil Conduct

  • Law IV: Freedom of Expression — Citizens may speak freely, provided their speech does not oppose the State or incite disorder.
  • Law V: Public Responsibility — All citizens are expected to contribute positively to society and uphold national dignity.

Imperial Authority

  • Law VI: Decrees of the Emperor — Imperial Decrees have the force of law and override any conflicting legislation.
  • Law VII: Obedience to the Crown — Resistance to or disobedience against Imperial directives shall be considered a criminal offense.
  • Law VIII: System of Justice — All rullings are final and may be changed by the Emperor
